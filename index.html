About

We are a family owned and operated business. Our company was established in 2015 and has been responsible for providing outstanding buffet catering ever since. Our specialty is Smoked BBQ and Italian but we offer a wide variety and are able to work with the clients wishes. Our regular customers particularly value our flexibility and prices. Not to mention our food! Our business serves the Windsor, Sedalia, Warrensburg, & Clinton area.
